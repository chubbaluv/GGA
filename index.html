The Global Gravity Association

A new vision for the International Downhill Federation

Organizing bodies for closed-road gravity sports have failed to meet the needs of the global community.
We are here to foster understanding and work together to craft solutions for the future.

Fluidity

Not just the boys

We love the worldâ€™s present practitioners and want to see the field flourish. That means actively making space for every gender, color and creed.

Not just brackets

All paying participants deserve their days of play whether or not they care for competition, We will offer top tier racing for those who want it amidst unfettered freeriding for all.

Not just boards

However you get your gravity giggles, we want to make a safe environment for you on the closed course. Blades, gravity bikes, drift trikes, luges, gravity cars and adaptive vehicles are all welcome.
